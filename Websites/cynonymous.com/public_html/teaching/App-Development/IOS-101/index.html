IOS 101: Crash Course Live
iOS Development

About this Course

Welcome to Crash Course Live: iOS App Development. In this course we will learn about the Xcode environment and the Swift language by building a fully deployable app together. This course is for beginners, no coding experience needed.

The idea of this course is to give us a flavor of what it’s like to develop an app by building a fairly complex app together. We are building a small version of the iPhone music player app, and then we will deploy it to our phones.

We will go from zero knowledge of building apps and programming to having the experience of developing a fully deployed app in about 3 hours.

The goal is to gain hands-on experience with app building so that we can apply that knowledge to our own apps. And most importantly we will have a lot of fun in the process!

What you will Learn

Section 1: Getting Started With Xcode 7 and Swift 2

  • Become comfortable working with the Xcode environment.
  • How to work with ViewController, Stroyboard, and IBOutlets.

Section 2: Swift 2 Programming Basics with Swift

  • How to use basic variables.
  • Basic object oriented programming.
  • Functions, arrays, conditionals, loops.

Section 3: Xcode UI Design

  • How to use Auto Layout and Constraints.
  • How to incorporate Assets into our projects.

Section 4: Putting it all together

  • Put it all together into our final media player app.

Prerequisites and Requirements
